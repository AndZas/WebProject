Drinks

Espresso

Strong black coffee, which is prepared in a coffee machine. Espresso has a pleasant aroma and a long aftertaste.

Cappuccino

An Italian coffee drink made on the basis of espresso with the addition of foamed milk heated to 65 degrees.

Frappuccino

This drink consists of a mixture of cold milk, strong coffee, sugar and chocolate syrup.

Hot chocolate

A warming and delicious drink made from cocoa powder, milk and various additives such as spices, vanilla or marshmallows.

Irish coffee

Hot alcoholic cocktail consisting of coffee, Irish whiskey, whipped cream and sugar. The cocktail has a creamy coffee flavor.

Vanilla latte

A delicious and flavorful drink that consists of espresso, milk and vanilla syrup. It has a delicate creamy taste.

Vanilla mocha

Sweet, fragrant and very tasty. Vanilla mocha is usually served with whipped cream and garnished with cinnamon or star anise.

Caramel mocha

Exquisite coffee drink that combines the rich aroma of strong espresso, the soft sweetness of caramel and gentle warm milk.

Sandwich
